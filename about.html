About Us

Welcome to Pay-Me , your trusted partner in secure transactions. At Pay-Me, we specialize in providing reliable escrow services tailored to meet your financial needs with confidence and peace of mind. Whether you're buying, selling, or transacting online, our platform ensures that funds are held securely until both parties are satisfied with the transaction's completion.

Our Vision

Is to become the go-to escrow service provider globally, ensuring that all parties in a transaction feel confident and secure.

Our Mission

To ensure that both buyers and sellers can trust in the safety and security of their payments.
We believe in creating a seamless experience for our users, so they can focus on their business without worrying about payment security.

Background

Pay-Me was founded in early 2024 by a group of tech enthusiasts as part of their capstone project at the Vephla University who recognized the growing need for secure, transparent, and efficient escrow services in the digital age. The founders aimed to create a platform that could provide an additional layer of trust in online transactions, especially in sectors like e-commerce, real estate, and freelance work, where large sums of money frequently change hands and disputes can be common.
