Getting started

Our Mission is to create a super flexible & easy mode of transaction between a buyer & a seller. Pay Me serves as the intermediary between both parties to safeguard funds and settle payment with ease.

Account Verification

The security of all our user’s funds is our topmost priority. It is important that every user verify their account before a transaction can be initiated to ensure a safe, smooth & easy transaction between both parties.

Tracking a Transaction

A Transaction ID serves as the code for tracking a transaction. A code will be sent for both parties for confirmation & verification before a payout can take place.

Dispute Resolution

If there is something wrong with the goods or service or you have not received a delivery, please contact your seller directly. If the seller does not respond to your requests, you can inform us within 14days of no response from the seller.
